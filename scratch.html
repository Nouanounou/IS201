I would define myself as someone that is always open to new experiences. Take a look at the brief list of things that I want to accomplish and tell the world about while I have the time to:

  • Start my own 5-star restaurant
  • Develop a charity/non-profit organization
  • Participate in a marathon
  • Star in movie/tv show
  • Jump out of an airplane
  • Go scuba diving
  • Visit landmarks such as: The Taj Mahal, The Eiffel Tower, Stonehenge, the Pyramids of Giza, and The Great Wall of China

  • Build a custom home for a family in need
  • Set foot on all 7 continents
  • Attend the World Cup

What are qualifiers for the items on my bucket list? Great question!

  1. They must be intellectually and culturally engaging
    • I want these items to become an outlet for fresh ideas and solutions for difficult problems in the world today
    • There's still so much that I'm eager to learn about other cultures, traditions, and mindsets of communities that exist all over the world. I want to be a life-long learner and a skilled international communicator
    • Every community, country, and corner in the world possesses value. Stepping out into the unknown and learning about the stories of people that go unseen is motivating and intriguing to me


  2. They should be cost effective and opportunities for me to positively benefit others
    • This could include volunteer opportunities to service projects while I travel abroad
    • Utilizing my talents and my time to lift someone else's budrens
    • Paying it forward to fund trips for other people
    • Enabling/encouraging companies and large organizations to volunteer in the community and give back as an intergrated ESG poposition in their business models


  3. These items should help me foster positive relationships that create lasting change for myself and my family
    • I am a firm advocate of persistent and intentional relationships
    • I never intend to travel alone. Taking my family along for the ride with me is a personal dream of mine!
    • I have a testimony of incredible people who have touched my life for good


  4. The most important point of all: Shameless fun!
    • Everyone needs a bucket list! Similar to checkpoints and other achievements, I believe that they degree to which we aspire to attain and cherish these bucket list items is in large part a reflection of the joy and pleasure that we should be seeking in our mortal experience!

My Mission Statement: A Life-Changing Experience

I strive to live my life and patter my choices after the two year mission that I served for the Church of Jesus-Christ of Latter Day Saints. I had invaluable experiences from the coasts of the Jersey shore and the rich red landscapes of the Ivory Coast in West Africa. They stretched me and expanded my capacity to love. I also gained a passion for design and cooking which have remained as important aspects of my life today. I wouldn't trade the hardships I labored through or the miracles I witnessed for anything.
